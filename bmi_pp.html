Privacy Policy – BMI Body Planner

Last updated: November 24, 2025

Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data is processed when using the BMI Body Planner application and how we ensure user safety.

1. What data do we collect?

The application does not collect any personal data from users. We do not gather, store, or process any information that could identify the user.

2. Data entered by the user

All information entered into the application, such as:

  • height
  • weight
  • age
  • gender
  • units (metric / imperial)
  • language

is processed exclusively locally on the user’s device. It is not uploaded to any server, stored online, or shared with third parties.

3. Ads and analytics

The application:

  • does not display ads,
  • does not use analytics tools,
  • does not integrate third-party services,
  • does not track users.

4. App permissions

The application does not require any permissions beyond those strictly necessary for basic operation. We do not access:

  • contacts
  • camera
  • microphone
  • location
  • files
  • or any other system data

5. Sharing data with third parties

The application does not share any personal data, as it does not collect any data.

6. Security

Because no data is stored or transmitted, there is no risk of a data breach or interception. The application is designed to operate fully locally and offline.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any new version will be published with an updated date.
