Privacy Policy – Home Bill Planner

Last updated: December 10, 2025

Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the Home Bill Planner application processes data and ensures user safety.

1. What data do we collect?

The application does not collect any personal data. We do not gather, store, or process information such as names, addresses, emails, phone numbers, or identifiers.

2. Data entered by the user

Users can enter and manage their own payment-related information, including:

  • bill type or category,
  • payment amount,
  • payment frequency,
  • payment due dates,
  • remarks or additional notes.

All entered data is stored locally on the user’s device using a private SQLite database. No data is uploaded, sent externally, or shared with any server or third party.

3. Local database (SQLite)

The application uses an internal SQLite database solely to store user-entered bill information. This data remains on the device and is not accessible to us or any external service.

4. Notifications

The app may ask for permission to send notifications. This permission is used only to:

  • remind users about upcoming payments,
  • improve usability and organization of bills.

Notification data is generated locally and never transmitted outside the device.

5. Ads and analytics

The application:

  • does not display ads,
  • does not use analytics tools,
  • does not collect usage statistics,
  • does not integrate third-party SDKs.

6. App permissions

The application does not request any permissions other than those required for sending notifications. It does not access:

  • contacts
  • camera
  • microphone
  • location
  • files or storage
  • system identifiers

7. Sharing data with third parties

The application does not share any data because no data is collected. Everything remains only on the user’s device.

8. Security

Since all data is kept locally, there is no risk of data interception or unauthorized access via network transfer. The application is designed to operate entirely offline.

9. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any new version will be published with an updated modification date.
